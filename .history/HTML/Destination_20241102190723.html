Fine Your Desierd Destinations

Waterfalls

Diyaluma Falls

  • Height

    • Second-highest waterfall in Sri Lanka, at 220 meters.

  • Natural Pools

    • Series of pools at different levels, perfect for a swim.

  • Hiking and Views

    • Short hike to the top with breathtaking views of the landscape.

  • Waterfall Picnic Spots

    • Popular for picnics due to its scenic beauty.

  • Photo Opportunities

    • Known for its dramatic cascade and beautiful rock formations.

Ravana Falls

  • Mythological Significance

    • Linked to the epic Ramayana, named after King Ravana.

  • Multi-Tiered Cascade

    • Falls over multiple layers of rock, creating a stunning effect.

  • Accessible Location

    • Located close to the town of Ella, making it easy to visit.

  • Swimming

    • Shallow pool at the base where visitors can take a dip.

  • Popular Tourist Spot

    • A must-visit attraction in the Ella area, with roadside viewing.

Bambarakanda Falls

  • Secluded Beach Experience

    • Tucked away and less crowded.

  • Clear Waters for Snorkeling

    • Ideal for snorkeling around coral reefs.

  • Scenic Nature Trails

    • Short trek to reach the beach through greenery.

  • Vibrant Marine Life

    • Rich underwater ecosystem.

  • Relaxing Atmosphere

    • Perfect for unwinding.

  • Beautiful Sunset Views

    • Scenic, quiet spot for sunsets.

Height – The tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka, reaching 263 meters. Surrounding Pine Forests – Enveloped by lush pine forests, enhancing its beauty. Seasonal Flow – Best viewed during the rainy season for a stronger flow. Hiking Trail – Trail leads close to the falls for a fantastic view. Scenic Picnic Area – Great spot for nature lovers to relax and enjoy the scenery.

Hikkaduwa

  • Coral Reefs and Snorkeling

    • Ideal for snorkeling around coral reefs.

  • Surfing

    • Steady waves, popular among surfers.

  • Beachfront Cafes and Nightlife

    • Bars and cafes with music and social scene.

  • Turtle Watching

    • Sea turtles can be seen near the shore.

  • Glass-Bottom Boat

    • View coral reefs from the boat.

  • Diving Spots

    • Underwater caves and wrecks to explore.

Beaches

Mirissa

  • Whale Watching

    • spot for blue whale and dolphin sightings.

  • Surfing & Snorkeling

    • Beginner-friendly waves and clear waters.

  • Beach Bars & Cafes

    • Lively atmosphere with fresh seafood and tropical drinks.

  • Secret Beach

    • A quieter, hidden gem nearby.

  • Picturesque Sunsets

    • Beautiful sunset views over the Indian Ocean.

Arugam Bay

  • World-Class Surfing

    • One of the best surf spots in Asia.

  • Laid-Back Vibe

    • Chill, bohemian atmosphere with beachside cafes.

  • Wildlife and Nature Excursions

    • Close to Kumana National Park.

  • Stunning Beaches and Landscapes

    • Scenic views at Elephant Rock and Pottuvil Lagoon.

  • Cultural Sites

    • Ancient temples like Muhudu Maha Viharaya.

  • Vibrant Nightlife

    • Lively social scene, especially during surf season.
