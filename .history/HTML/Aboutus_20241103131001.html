About Us

Travel World is a travel agency that provides the best travel experience for you.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: At Travel World, we are dedicated to offering unique and memorable travel experiences across Sri Lanka, celebrating the island's rich culture, stunning landscapes, and unmatched hospitality. We focus on quality service, customer satisfaction, and eco-friendly tourism practices to ensure each journey with us not only delights our travelers but also respects the local environment and communities.

Vision: Our vision is to become Sri Lanka’s trusted travel partner, helping clients from around the globe discover the island’s beauty in an authentic and sustainable way. We strive to create lasting memories and connections that inspire our guests to cherish and preserve Sri Lanka's wonders.

Our Story

Travel World was born out of a passion for showcasing Sri Lanka’s unique charm and diversity to the world. Founded in 2020 by a team of travel enthusiasts, our agency started with small, curated tours around cultural hotspots and scenic destinations. Over the years, we’ve grown into a reputable agency known for personalized service and unique travel experiences. We are proud to have served over 2,000 travelers, with many becoming repeat customers who share our love for Sri Lanka.

Some of our proudest milestones include receiving the Eco-Friendly Tourism Award in 2022, and partnering with local artisans and eco-lodges to promote responsible tourism across the island. Every step of our journey reflects our commitment to providing high-quality, enriching travel experiences.

Why Choose Us?

  • Customized Travel Packages: We understand that each traveler is unique. Our team designs personalized itineraries that align with your interests, whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, cultural immersion, or wildlife encounters.
  • Experienced Guides: Our guides are locals with extensive knowledge of Sri Lanka’s history, culture, and hidden gems. They’re not just guides but storytellers, committed to making each trip insightful and unforgettable.
  • Community Partnerships: We believe in supporting the communities we visit. Travel World collaborates with local artisans, small businesses, and eco-friendly lodges, helping to uplift communities while offering you an authentic experience.
  • Eco-Conscious Practices: Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. From promoting reusable travel items to organizing beach clean-ups, we strive to reduce our footprint and inspire travelers to be eco-conscious too.
